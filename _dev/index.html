Hello!

My name is Valerio Pierbattista and I have a passion for building things on the web.

I am a creative Front End Developer and Web Designer living in Rome, Italy. I love simple and functional interfaces optimized for anything that can access the web.

I'm passionate about content-centric responsive webdesign and I know a thing or two about html, css, presentational javascript, build tools and pre-processors. I worked in both small and big companies, from startups to large multinationals in both local and international environments.

I'm currently a Front-End Developer at DOING where I develop websites for an ever expanding number of clients including Eni, Telepass, ALD, Enjoy and more.

In my spare time, I like to build stupid side projects with colleagues and friends. You can read more about me on my online résumé.
